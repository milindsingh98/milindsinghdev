Optimized printer waiting time for domestic and educational purposes


The project deals with the domestic convenience and educational services-
- An Android application that will ask the user to upload the file he/she wants to get printed. Then the app will ask the format including margin, font, font size etc.
- The document is then uploaded to the cloud from where encapsulating with the requirements it is sent to the print hub where actual printing is done.
- The file is deleted from the cloud once the job is completed.
- The estimated waiting time is calculated taking in considerations with the traffic, the waiting time and the printing time.
- It makes printing efficient as the work is up to mark and avoids the Application version printing errors.
- The account handling of the print center is simplified and the users pay for what they get.

Efficient Garage Collection Bins and Garbage Management



The project is a total reformation of the garbage collection and a new model which is optimized to collect the garbage.

- The garbage bins have a new design of the trapezoid. It has a Radio Frequency transmitter which transmits the radio signal to the Collection hub.
- Since the dustbins positions are stationary each radio transmitter is fixed at a position and when the dustbin is full. It sends the signal.
- The signals are collected and the coordinates are put in the shortest path algorithm to find the shortest path for the collection truck.
- This way it saves up a lot of fuel, avoids the overflowing trash bins,
- Using ML, A trend between the data can be set and a schedule can be formed for collection of garbage.

Abstract—The waste management in India has not changed a lot in past decades. The collection bins, collection method and disposal have been quite same. This paper proposes a system which gives a new design, placement of garbage bins. The connection and communication of all the bins to the base station. The location of the dustbins are kept stationary and all the location are stored at the base center along with the unique bin ID’s. The transceivers are placed on every dustbin which transmits the signal to the base station which indicated whether the dustbin is full or not. The routing algorithms are used to find the shortest path which connects to all the dustbins. The collection trucks follows the optimized route for facilitation and it saves fuel , brings accordance to the system.

I. INTRODUCTION
The Garbage collection bins which are used by the municipal corporation often comes in the same pattern. Since couple of decades there has been talks about the efficient garbage management and disposal but the garbage collection bins have not changed much in the year. The structure has been the same in the years. There have been garbage collection[4] trucks with the fork lifters to reduce the manual work. The problem which still is stagnant in India is not the garbage but the garbage bins and the sizes which they come in. We all have noticed the spillage of garbage outside of the bins. The question arises who is responsible for it? In this paper we mention the improvised design of the dustbins and how they can be made more efficient. To find the answers to the question “Why do people still litter garbage outside dustbin rather than putting in the bins?” There were a lot of answers I got from the people that I would like to mention in this paper. • The height of the dustbins that makes people vulnerable to throwing heavy garbage bags into the bins. • The typical smell that comes from the dustbins. • The overflowing bins which make them to throw the garbage nearby. • In rains the dustbins are filled with water and there is no space left for the garbage. • There is no optimized path on which the collection truck collects and moreover the base station does not know whether and when the dustbins are full.

I. PROPOSED SYSTEM The proposed system is the new design of the dustbins and it will cover all the problems which are faced by the people. The basic structure of the dustbins will be of the inverted trapezoid. The inverted trapezoid is chosen because the surface area is larger at the bigger end and it can hold more garbage than the cube and cuboidal bins. The inverted bin will be covered with a hemisphere which will cover one side of the dustbin such that if someone even aims into the dustbins than it doesn’t fall off from the other end. The height of the dustbins can’t be trimmed but to make the dustbins feasible they can be placed up to 2-2.5ft. in the ground. They can be still emptied and lifted by the fork lifter trucks. The concrete walls of the ground will prevent the moisture and damage to the dustbins. A rubber ring will be placed on the top such that there is no direct contact of the dustbins with the ground and placing rubber ring will also ensure that during the emptying of the dustbins the walls are intact and are not damaged and the dustbins will also be in intact shape. The proposed rubber ring will also provide the seal which would not allow the rain water to enter below the bins. The hemisphere covers on the dustbins will not let the rain water to stagnate in bins. The smell that comes out of the dustbins will also be reduced by the lid. The lid will be detachable so that while the garbage collection the lid can be removed and the garbage can be easily loaded into the trucks for dumping. The foul smells that comes out of the dustbins can be lowered by using epoxy resins and the dustbins inner walls will be painted will Hydrophobic paints so that nothing sticks on the wall of the container. The trucks are fitted with the The sensors are attached to the dustbin to check when the dustbin is full and the signal is sent when the dustbin is at 75% capacity to consider the time of commute by the collection trucks and routing. Raspberry pie is used to create the circuit. The photosensor is used to maintain the circuit closed between the laser and the senor. As the garbage falls, the circuit is broken and the timer of 100 seconds start. If the circuit is restored then the timer stops. If the circuit does not get closed even after 100 seconds then the RF transceiver is started. The status signal of the dustbin is sent along with the dustbin ID. As the transceiver transmits the signal, the whole sensor system goes in battery saver mode, until the dustbin is emptied. The sensor uses rechargeable battery which theoretically should last for a month. The Existing dustbins do not have any sensors and are neither connected through anyways to the base stations. The Proposed system provides the connection to each and every dustbin. To provide the connections to each and every dustbin we are using the radio frequency[3] waves. The system is based on the GoTenna technology developed in U.S. for SOS services. It uses the unlicensed frequency bandwidth to transmit the information from the user device to the base stations. The TCP/IP protocol and OSPF are being used for transmitting and securing the data. The radio frequency transceiver will transmit outboard messages to the base stations. The location of the dustbins will be pre-stored at the base station and every dustbin will get a unique id along with the location co-ordinated. The signal is sent on unlicensed Radio spectrum[1]. The unlicensed low frequency bandwidth available in India is 865MHz to 867MHz. The purpose of using low frequency bandwidth is that they don’t interfere with the Mobile communication and other bandwidth. The Transceiver is connected to the Raspberry pie and an antenna allows the transmission of the signal. The battery of the transceiver in theories should last for 15-20 days. The signal includes the unique ID of the dustbin. As the signal reaches the base station, The ID is recorded in the table and then routing algorithm is applied. Process flow diagram for sending signal The base station receives the ID’s pulls out the location of the dustbins by ID. The location co-ordinates then are mapped and shortest path[2] calculation is used. Firstly, convex hull is used to map the outline and give the approximate distance to each destination and Dijkstra’s algorithm is used to find the distance keeping the starting location as the base station then the shortest path which connected to all the dustbins which are full are calculated and the path is followed. This optimized route saves fuel and also increases the efficiency of the system. From the statistics the system can further predict where the demand is more and where it is less and thus leading to more effective routing.

There are further division of classes of division in location API package to retrieve the location information from the user.  LocationManager- This class provides access to the location services as well as provides facility to get the best location provider as per the Criteria.  LocationListner- It provides the callback methods which are provided when the location is changed. The listener object is registered within the location manager class.  LocationProvider- It’s an abstract super class for location providers. A location provides the periodic information of the geographic location of the device.  Criteria – It provides the application choose the suitable location provider by providing access to set of required properties of the Location Provider. In this application we will use the API provided by google maps. With the help of the google maps we can provide the device’s more accurate information and it Google maps API also gives the notification to the customer about the mall they entered and provide with the internal map of the map .This will help us to optimize our application and work it more efficiently. The database will store the following information within it.  A table will store all the information about the customer which will include their NAME, MOBILE NUMBER, DATE OF BIRTH, E-MAIL ID, COUNTRY, STATE, CITY, PINCODE.  Another table will contain all the data of the shops and outlets in the mall. This table will store their SHOP/OUTLET NAME, PASSWORD, ADDRESS, NAME OF MALL, LOCATION (it will be fixed),CONTACT NUMBER,GSTIN NUMBER,PAN NUMBER.  The deals and discount provided by the clients are stored in another table which has fields like category, type , price range , percentage of discount , validity of the discount , clock timer , products(optional) on discount, brands , terms and conditions , images2 , image of the product , price of the product , acceptance of payment mode . This table is directly connected to the homepage of the application and the information stored here will be displayed there in the proper alignment and proper presentation elements.
